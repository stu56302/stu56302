About Us

For generations here at St. Guardians Primary we have guided the most promising little light bearers from all corners of the galaxy to become wielders of the light, protectors of what remains of humanity throughout the solar system.

Our little light bearers are chosen by the Traveller, the light within them is nurtured in our early years preschool, teaching them the ways in which we contribute to and care for, what remains of our society.

Light bearers live to well over 300 years old so for their first 30 years they will travel through their educational journey with us here at St. Guardians. Through the primary years they will receive training in all three Guardian disciplines, Hunter, Warlock and Titan.

In a light bearers final years at St. Guardians Primary School, they will discover the natural path that the Traveller has chosen for them, their skills will have been proven and therefore show which discipline is their calling.

St Guardians Primary School will develop the next generation of light bearers and prepare them fully for their apprentice years as an underling Guardian.
