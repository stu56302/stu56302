A message from our Principal

Hello, my name is Mr. Cayde Six and for the last five years I have been the Principal at St. Guardians. In this time, my team and I have taken the school out of special measures and up to an outstanding rating from Ofsted. It has been an honour and a pleasure to teach our young Guardians the power of the light. With a strong team of leaders with excellent teaching skills we will ensure all our young Guardians receive the best of starts in their schooling journey.
