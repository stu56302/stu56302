Governers

Our governing body ensures the operations at St. Guardians run smoothly and ethically. We pride ourselves on our long history of developing the greatest of Guardians. We endeavour to always do right and bring light back to the universe. Our board of governors are made up of Suraya Hawthorn, Tess Everis, Master Rahool, Amanda Holliday and our Chairperson Eva Levante.
